Greek Salad

Description:

Greek salad is a well known Greek dish consisting of sliced cucumbers, tomatoes, green and red bell peppers, red onion, olives, feta cheese, and topped with an olive oil base dressing. We will show you how to prepare a good greek salad so that you know how to.


Ingredients:

  • 1 head romaine lettuce - rinsed, dried and chopped
  • 1 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 (6 ounce) can pitted black olives
  • 1 green bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 red bell pepper, chopped
  • 2 large tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 1 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 6 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 lemon, juiced
  • ground black pepper

Directions

  1. In a large salad bowl, combine the chopped Romaine, onion, olives, bell peppers, tomatoes, cucumber and feta.
  2. Whisk the olive oil, oregano, lemon juice, and black pepper to make the dressing.
  3. Pour dressing over salad and toss it.
  4. Serve.
